COVID-19 Prevention Tips

Protect yourself and others from COVID-19 by following these guidelines.

  • Wear a mask in public places.
  • Maintain at least 6 feet of physical distance.
  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water.
  • Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol.
  • Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.
  • Stay home if you're feeling unwell or have COVID-19 symptoms.
  • Follow local health guidelines and restrictions.
  • Wear a mask in public places.

    Wearing a mask helps reduce the spread of respiratory droplets that may contain the virus.
